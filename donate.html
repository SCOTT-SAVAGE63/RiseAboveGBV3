Tax Benefit Information

Section 18A Tax Certificates

Rise Above GBVF is a registered Non-Profit Organization (NPO) and Public Benefit Organization (PBO). Your donations qualify for tax deductions under Section 18A of the Income Tax Act.

‚úÖ For Individuals

Claim up to 10% of your taxable income as a deduction for donations to approved PBOs.

‚úÖ For Companies

Deductions up to 10% of taxable income for corporate social investment donations.

‚úÖ Certificate Delivery

Section 18A certificates are emailed within 7 working days of your donation.

Requirements for Tax Deduction:

  • Donation must be made in cash (not goods or services)
  • Valid Section 18A certificate must be issued
  • Certificate must be retained for SARS submission
  • Donations must be to approved Public Benefit Organizations
