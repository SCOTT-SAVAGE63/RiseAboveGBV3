Volunteer Requirements & Process

To ensure the safety and effectiveness of our programs, all volunteers must complete the following process:

1

Application & Screening

Complete our volunteer application form and participate in an initial screening interview.

2

Background Check

All direct service volunteers must complete a criminal background check for safety.

3

Comprehensive Training

Complete our mandatory training program covering GBVF awareness, safety, and role-specific skills.

4

Supervised Onboarding

Begin your role with close supervision and ongoing support from experienced team members.

Frequently Asked Questions

Common questions about volunteering with Rise Above GBVF

Do I need prior experience to volunteer?

+

No prior experience is required for most roles! We provide comprehensive training for all volunteers. What's most important is your passion for helping others and willingness to learn.

How much time do I need to commit?

+

We offer flexible commitments ranging from 2 hours per week for event volunteers to 8+ hours for regular roles. We'll work with you to find a schedule that fits your availability.

Is training provided?

+

Yes! All volunteers complete our comprehensive training program covering GBVF awareness, safety protocols, communication skills, and role-specific training. Training is free and scheduled at convenient times.

Can students volunteer?

+

Absolutely! We welcome students and can provide documentation for community service hours. Many of our roles are perfect for students studying social work, psychology, or community development.

What support will I receive as a volunteer?

+

You'll receive ongoing supervision, regular check-ins, additional training opportunities, peer support, and access to counseling resources if needed. We're committed to supporting our volunteers.

Are there remote volunteering opportunities?

+

Yes! We have several remote roles including administrative support, social media assistance, research, and content creation. These roles offer flexibility while making a meaningful impact.
