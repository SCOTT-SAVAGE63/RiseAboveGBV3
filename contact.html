Frequently Asked Questions

Quick answers to common questions about getting help

Is my information confidential?

+

Yes, absolutely. All communications with Rise Above GBVF are completely confidential. We prioritize your privacy and safety above all else.

How quickly will someone respond to my message?

+

We respond to all non-emergency messages within 24 hours. For urgent matters, please call our crisis line for immediate assistance.

Do I need to pay for your services?

+

All our support services are completely free of charge. We believe help should be accessible to everyone regardless of financial situation.

Can I remain anonymous?

+

Yes, you can choose to remain anonymous when contacting us. We respect your decision and will still provide the support you need.

Need to Talk Right Now?

Our trained support team is available 24/7 to provide immediate help, safety planning, and emotional support.
